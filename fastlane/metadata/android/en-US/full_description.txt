AvoCook is a free and open source recipe notebook for Android and iOS.

It works offline without an account, and can also synchronize with a Nextcloud Cookbook instance.

Main features:

* local recipes, categories and photos
* optional Nextcloud Cookbook sync
* recipe import from schema.org Recipe pages
* serving adjustment
* cooking timers with local notifications
* PDF export and printing
* JSON backup and restore

AvoCook is an independent app and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Nextcloud GmbH.
